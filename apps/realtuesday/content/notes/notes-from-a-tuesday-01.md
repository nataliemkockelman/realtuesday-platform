## why tuesday.

Tuesday is the day nobody pays attention to. *Monday gets the gym memberships. Friday gets the cocktails.* Tuesday just shows up and does the work.

That's the energy. This isn't a hustle newsletter. It's not a wellness newsletter. It's a Tuesday newsletter — for people who already know what they're doing and just want better tools.

> "Tuesday just shows up and does the work. That's the whole brand."

## what's coming.

Every week: one short essay, one tool worth knowing about, one thing I shipped or learned. Sometimes a recipe. Probably some swearing.

No tracking. No referral programs. No "5 morning habits of billionaires." Just notes.
